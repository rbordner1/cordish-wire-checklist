Step 2 of 2 · Initiator
Initiator Checklist
Complete all items. Once submitted, Jon Cordish will receive a secure link to the SharePoint record for final approval.
Call 1 — Verify the Relationship Contact
  • Independently verified Contact 1 via Google or LinkedIn I looked up the relationship contact provided by the Requestor and confirmed their name, title, and employer match a publicly listed profile — before making any call.
    ⚠ Required for all vendors. Critical step for new vendors and banking changes.
  • Called Contact 1 and confirmed they are a real employee at this vendor I called the relationship contact at the number provided by the Requestor and verbally confirmed they work at the vendor in the stated role.
  • Contact 1 confirmed the AP / wire verification contact (Contact 2) During the call with Contact 1, they verbally confirmed the name and phone number of the person in their accounting or AP department who can verify the wire instructions.
Call 2 — Verify the Banking Details
  • W-9 obtained and legal name / EIN cross-referenced A W-9 is on file or has been received. The legal name on the W-9 matches the account name submitted by the Requestor and confirmed by Contact 2. The EIN has been recorded.
    ⚠ Required for all new vendors and any banking changes. Known vendors — confirm W-9 is already on file.
  • Called Contact 2 (AP / wire verification contact) as referred by Contact 1 I called the vendor's AP contact using the number confirmed by Contact 1 — not a number from the invoice or payment request — and reached the correct person.
    ⚠ Insurance requirement: banking details verified via a call not sourced from the payment request.
  • Contact 2 verbally confirmed bank name, routing number, and account number The banking details confirmed by Contact 2 match exactly what was submitted by the Requestor. Any discrepancy was escalated — I did not proceed.
    ⚠ Insurance requirement: call-back verification of all banking details before wire entry.
  • Wire entered in M&T exactly as verbally confirmed Routing number, account number, account name, and amount entered in M&T Treasury Center match what Contact 2 confirmed — not solely what appears on the invoice.
  • Wire staged but NOT released — pending Jon's approval The wire is set up in M&T but has not been submitted or released. It will not be released until Jon Cordish approves.
    ⚠ Insurance requirement: Executive approval required before release.
  • No unusual urgency or pressure applied This request was not submitted under pressure or with a directive to bypass normal verification steps.
